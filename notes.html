Libraries are the original open information. Transforming lives through access to information: what we're about.
Increasingly, that information is mediated through code. Libraries' role in engaging citizens as active media participants and in teaching digital literacy is conditioned by our own understanding of, and control over, the software we use to provide services.

In the ideal world, many librarians would have the programming skills to customize this software in ways that make sense for their workflows and their patrons. Some librarians have done amazing work in this direction. and there are lots of great open source projects...
